Fundamental Portfolio Manager (FPM)

FPM is a powerful, flexible, and scalable investment management solution designed to streamline operations and enhance decision-making for portfolio managers.

Key Features

  • Multi-Asset Support: Manage equities, fixed income, derivatives, and more.
  • Real-Time Valuation: Accurate, up-to-date portfolio insights.
  • Compliance & Reporting: Built-in tools for regulatory and client reporting.
  • Integration Ready: Seamlessly connects with your existing systems.
